## 🏢 About The Company

**Steel Defence Freelance & Consulting Ltd** (Company No. 13208126) is a specialised IT and Cybersecurity consultancy based in London.

We pivot the traditional recruitment model by replacing "sales-focused recruiters" with **active technical practitioners**. We partner with CTOs and Heads of Engineering to technically validate candidates for high-stakes roles in:

* **Artificial Intelligence & ML** (Azure AI, Computer Vision, NLP)
* **Cybersecurity** (Red Team Ops, DFIR, Threat Hunting)
* **SaaS Architecture** (Cloud Native, Scalable Infrastructure)

Our mission is to eliminate the risk of bad technical hires by ensuring every candidate is vetted with great technical depth.

---

## 🛠 Services Overview

We offer three primary engagement models for technical hiring:

1. **Retained Search:** Full-cycle recruitment where we act as your internal technical hiring partner.
2. **Technical Vetting as a Service (TVaaS):** You provide the candidates; we provide the technical interview and "Go/No-Go" validation.
3. **Talent Pipeline Advisory:** Strategic consulting on job descriptions, salary benchmarking, and market mapping for AI/Cyber roles.
